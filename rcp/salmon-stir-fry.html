This was a dish I originally created for my girlfriend's birthday lunch - she likes salmon, and she likes stir-fry, so it was a no-brainer. I made some slight changes this time around, and unfortunately it didn't quite live up to how good it was the first time, but that's probably nostalgia and sleep deprivation speaking...

The wok-hei from the stir-fry technique makes the veg and noodles really stand out, and brings out the best in the fermented sauces typical in East-Asian cooking. The grilled salmon is the stand-out centrepiece of this dish, though, with the smoked paprika adding a subtle twang amidst the decadent sweet and umami flavours of the glaze.

Ingredients

Serves four

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • Light soy sauce
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Honey
  • Smoked paprika
  • 4 Nests of egg noodles
  • 1 Medium onion
  • 2 Cloves of garlic
  • Fresh ginger
  • 2 Thai red chillis
  • 1 Leek
  • 2 Carrots
  • Oyster sauce
  • Dark soy sauce
  • Fish sauce
